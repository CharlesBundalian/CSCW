Download the Game!

Being honest here, Edda Café is one of the greatest Visual Novels I have ever played. It was made in a month with heartfelt passion for the Love Game Jam, it's story is fairly short but it's more than enough to get me crying for a good while. The quality from the amazing art and music you've seen here are just splendid, I recommend it to anyone and everyone in need of some magic to give it a try.
