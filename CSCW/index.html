Welcome to Edda Café!

Have you ever heard the legend about the cafe that can turn back time? A magic chair that can send you to another world? Magical sweets that can change your fate? If there's a moment in your past that you want to go back to, maybe you should give it a try. But... Who really believes that stuff? I don't like mystical and uncertain things like that. There's no scientific data to prove any of it. It's all just trickery commercial schemes by the industry to attract customers and empty their wallets. It's just marketing for money, pure and simple.

And yet... Why is my heart tickled?

What is Edda?

To any normal person Edda is just any normal café with delicious drinks and pastries that have awfully weird names, but to those who need it the most, Edda café is a place of magic. A place to mend broken hearts, although our food can take you back in time it also starts the flow of time towards the future to those who have given up and put a pause in their life.

Making Our Food

Here at Edda Café we only serve the best, we observe basic guidelines when it comes to safety and culinary expertise! Our magic isn't only limited to healing hearts, It's about the love our chefs put into them that truly heal both heart and stomach by the end of your visit! So you can be sure what you eat here is not just magical in properties but on your tastebuds too!

Food

Our food is named after the experiences of our past customers and what pastry best represents their situation the best, they're known to be unique and sweet much like who and what it was named after. The greatest magic is the delicious taste that truly encapsulates the moment and takes you to another world while on the edge of your seat!

Drinks

From mystical tears to unforgotten memories, our brews will take you back to a time your heart has always desired. Does your heart ache? Does it gleam with joy? Edda knows, it's in the cup we're brewing for you right now. When the coffee drips, the magic starts to happen. Once it's done it's your choice if you want to come back and enjoy your drink.
